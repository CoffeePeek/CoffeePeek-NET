<objective>
Enable YARP active health checks on all four clusters and add rate limiting policy to shops-Map-route. Remove the IsDevelopment() guard that prevents /health from being registered in Production.

Purpose: YARP has ConsecutiveFailures health check policy already configured but disabled ("Enabled": false). The /health endpoint is only registered in Development (IsDevelopment() guard in ServiceDefaults). Enabling health checks without removing the guard would cause YARP to probe /health in Production, receive 404 on all clusters, mark all destinations unhealthy, and shed all traffic (complete 502 failure). Both sides MUST be fixed in the same execution. The shops-Map-route has no rate limiting policy — YARP does not enforce the global limiter on a route unless a RateLimiterPolicy is set explicitly.

Output: Gateway appsettings.json with Enabled:true on all 4 clusters and RateLimiterPolicy:global on shops-Map-route; ServiceDefaults/Extensions.cs with unconditional MapHealthChecks calls.
</objective>
